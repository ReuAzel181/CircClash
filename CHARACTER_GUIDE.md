- Enemies can avoid the initial projectile with good movement
- Must balance between normal shots and saving stacks for optimal timing
- Requires timing for maximum damage effectiveness
- No particular specialization beyond boomerang mechanics
- Medium range limits effectiveness against dedicated snipers

## üõ°Ô∏è Steel Guardian
**Class:** Shield Sentinel Tank  
**Element:** Metal

### Stats
- **Health:** 450 HP (Highest in game!)
- **Attack:** 45 DMG (Heavy energy wave damage)
- **Defense:** 100 DEF (25% damage reduction)
- **Speed:** 40 SPD (Heavier but steady)
- **Range:** 400 units (Extended energy wave reach)

### Special Ability: Energy Wave Surge
**Description:** Stops to charge metallic energy in shields for 1 second, then unleashes a massive energy wave that grows larger as it travels, dealing damage over time and slowing all enemies it passes through  
**Bullet Style:** Expanding energy wave with enhanced radius and continuous damage  
**Tactical Role:** Ultimate defensive tank with extended area denial and crowd control

**Attack Behavior:** All basic attacks now execute Energy Wave Surge; primary attack is unified to the special ability.

### Energy Wave Mechanics
- **1-second charge period** where Guardian becomes stationary and glows with energy (reduced from 1.5s)
- **Enhanced growing wave** that expands rapidly as it travels (1.8x growth rate)
- **Larger starting size** (20 unit radius) for immediate area coverage
- **Extended travel distance** up to 400 units for map-wide control
- **45 damage** initial impact plus **15 damage per tick** over time
- **3-second duration** for damage over time effect
- **40% movement speed reduction** applied to all enemies hit
- **4-second slow duration** hampering enemy positioning and escapes
- **Faster charging effects** with intensified energy sparks and growing aura

### Desperation Mode (Below 50% Health)
- **0.5-second charge time** (half normal charge duration)
- **500 projectile speed** (double normal wave speed)
- **Red energy effects** indicating desperate fury
- **Intensified visual effects** with more sparks and stronger glow
- **Enhanced threat level** making low-health Steel Guardian extremely dangerous

### Tank Specializations
- **Damage Reduction:** Takes 25% less damage from all sources
- **Self-Repair:** Regenerates 2 HP per second when damaged
- **Heavy Armor:** Increased mass and knockback resistance
- **Energy Mastery:** Channels metallic energy for devastating area attacks

### Strengths
- Massive health pool (450 HP vs normal ~260 HP)
- Exceptional survivability with damage reduction and healing
- Energy wave provides massive area control and crowd control
- Damage over time creates sustained pressure on multiple enemies
- Slow effect disrupts enemy positioning and retreat options
- Charging visual warning allows for tactical counterplay
- Self-sustaining in prolonged fights with regeneration
- **Desperation mode** makes Guardian extremely dangerous when wounded
- **Faster attacks when low** create comeback potential

### Weaknesses
- Completely vulnerable during 1-second charge period (reduced exposure)
- Telegraph attack gives enemies time to reposition
- Stationary during charge makes Guardian easy target
- Limited to forward-facing wave direction
- Requires strategic timing and positioning for maximum effectiveness

- **High survivability** with substantial health pool and combo protection

- **Predictable extension pattern** can be anticipated and avoided

## Tactical Analysis

### Character Tiers (Power Rankings)

**S-Tier (Strongest Overall)**
- Void Sniper (High risk, extreme reward with precision shots)
- Blade Master (Devastating close-range potential)
- Iron Titan (Buffed into dominance with devastating damage) *(Recently Buffed)*

**A-Tier (Very Strong)**
- Lightning Striker (Excellent balance of speed and damage)
- Shadow Assassin (Superior mobility and damage)
- Wind Archer (Excellent range and sustained damage)

**B-Tier (Balanced)**
- Fire Warrior (Balanced dual-shot area control) *(Recently Balanced)*
- Steel Guardian (Excellent tank capabilities)
- Plasma Vortex (Solid all-around performance)

**C-Tier (Situational)**
- Chaos Bomber (Area control specialist)
- Ice Knight (Strong but slow)
- Mystic Orb (Nerfed but still viable)

### Combat Roles

**Tanks:** Steel Guardian, Ice Knight, Iron Titan  
**DPS:** Lightning Striker, Blade Master, Void Sniper  
**Assassins:** Shadow Assassin, Lightning Striker  
**Support:** Mystic Orb, Wind Archer  
**Area Control:** Fire Warrior, Chaos Bomber, Ice Knight

### Counter Strategies

- **vs Tanks:** Use hit-and-run tactics with fast characters
- **vs Snipers:** Close distance quickly with high-speed assassins
- **vs Assassins:** Use tanky characters to absorb damage
- **vs Area Control:** Maintain distance and use precise attacks
